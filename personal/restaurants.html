Mount Rushmore of Restaurants

A "Mount Rushmore" is a list of the four best elements of a particular category, in this case, restaurants. These are my top four restaurants that I would recommend to anyone, anywhere, anytime. They are not ranked in any particular order.

  • Ratna Cafe

    Adyar, Chennai

    My dad's favorite hometown cafe, probably the best sambar in the world, the vada and idli sambar are worth a trip.

  • Magari by Oca

    Vancouver, BC

    A pasta tasting menu. Seven courses. No phone, no reservation, no menu. Show up at open. Oh, and get the Cannanau.

  • Is that French

    Vancouver, BC

    So, this place has no kitchen. But the summer menu is always great and the Basque cheesecake is yummy.

  • Little Collins

    Amsterdam, Netherlands

    The sister location for brunch/breakfast in De Pijp, they have a rotating menu but the yogurt sucuk bowl was the best breakfast I have had in a while.
